# Video Assets for Brew&Bloom

## Background Videos
Place your video files here for the homepage background:

### Required files:
- `boba-background.mp4` - Main background video (H.264 format)
- `boba-background.webm` - Alternative format for better browser support

### Recommended specifications:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080px (Full HD minimum)
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **File size**: Under 10MB for good performance
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) as primary, WebM as fallback
- **Audio**: None (video should be muted)

### Video sources you can use:
- Stock video sites: Pexels, Unsplash, Pixabay (free)
- Premium: Shutterstock, Getty Images, Adobe Stock
- Search terms: "bubble tea making", "coffee brewing", "tea preparation", "boba pearls"

### Example free video sources:
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/bubble%20tea/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/coffee%20brewing/

### How to add videos:
1. Download your chosen video
2. Rename it to `boba-background.mp4`
3. Place it in this `/public/videos/` directory
4. Optionally convert to WebM format for better compression

The video will automatically appear as the homepage background!
